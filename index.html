DM BANK AD, SKOPJE

DM BANK is one of the biggest and strongest brand on the Macedonian financial market.

With its 26 years’ history as a financial institution in Macedonia, here’s where DM BANK AD Skopje stands today:

  • We are fourth largest bank on the market by assets, being 668, 3 mill EUR.
  • We are present all over the country, offering services to our clients through our wide branch network that includes 13 branches.
  • We have over 500 employees.
  • Our ATMs network includes 140 ATMs
  • We have widely distributed POS terminals over our trade partners, counting 6.385 in total.
  • We serve the country’s nation with a broad range of financial services, including personal banking, credit cards, housing loans, consumer loans, savings and investments, business loans, domestic and foreign payment processing.
  • We are a leader in offering tailored banking services; introducing new technologies on the financial market; providing fast service through the high developed and modern alternative channel platform.
